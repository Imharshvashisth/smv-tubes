SS Round Tubes

Engineered for high-pressure applications and flawless structural integrity. Our stainless steel round tubes deliver raw power with unparalleled precision.

ROUND TUBES
Flagship Product

Unyielding Strength,
Flawless Execution.

Manufactured using high-frequency induction welding and bright annealing processes, our Stainless Steel Round Tubes are designed to withstand extreme environments, immense pressure, and corrosive elements. Perfect for both heavy industrial usage and premium architectural finishes.

  • Exceptional Corrosion Resistance: Grade 304/316/316L ensures longevity in harsh chemical and marine environments.
  • High Tensile Integrity: Subjected to rigorous flaring, flattening, and hydrostatic testing.
  • Mirror-Like Finish: Multi-head polishing guarantees aesthetic perfection for architectural needs.
  • Zero Tolerance Dimensions: Absolute precision in outer diameter and wall thickness.
